# Clarify Skill

When to use: requirements are ambiguous, missing parameters, or success criteria are unclear.

Core instructions:
- Ask at most 2 clarifying questions, then proceed.
- Focus on: goal, constraints, data sources, success metrics.
- Return a concise summary of clarifications before acting.

Template:
1. Goal: what is the desired outcome?
2. Constraints: limits (time, data, permissions).
3. Data: sources and quality expectations.
4. Success: how will we know it's done?
